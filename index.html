Welcome to the Flappy Bird Extravaganza: Where Learning is a Flap Away!

Why Flappy Bird is Educational

  • Flappy Bird isn't just your average bird-brained game; it's a flight of fancy into the world of coding!
  • With Pocket Code at its wingtips, Flappy Bird turns coding into a hilarious adventure where even birds can learn to program!
  • Ever dreamed of mastering multiple languages? Well, Flappy Bird's got you covered with support for 13 tongues! You'll be chirping in Chinese and squawking in Spanish in no time!
  • Need to brush up on your communication skills? Share your scores with friends through a user-friendly web page and watch the flock gather!

Features of Flappy Bird

  • Say goodbye to boring scoreboards! Flappy Bird's scoreboard is so fancy, it's practically wearing a top hat and monocle!
  • Customize your gaming experience with more colors than a peacock's feather collection! Go wild and make those pipes pop!
  • Explore background modes that will make your gaming experience more dynamic than a flock of flamingos on a rollercoaster!
  • With advanced sliders and tap-and-hold functionality, you'll feel like a coding maestro, conducting your birdie symphony!

Why Pocket Code Rocks

  • Pocket Code is the ultimate wingman for unleashing your creative genius! With its drag-and-drop interface, even a pigeon can learn to code!
  • Who needs a nest when you have Pocket Code? It's your one-stop-shop for creating interactive stories, games, and animations that will make your friends flock to you!
  • Dive into the Catrobat community and join the coding party! It's like a bird feeder for your brain, filled with ideas and inspiration!
